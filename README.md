<h1> The Technology Investment Plan of a Small Publishing House </h1>
<p> A small publishing house decided to upgrade from a shared commercial cloud to an in-house main server system. The company has also grown by several people.  The staff in each department has been provided with a standard desktop tower, monitor and mouse. </p>  <p>
  
</p>
<p> Each department has been allocated a development budget for their specific technology needs</p>
<p> Each department has also been allocated funds for their technological wants, above the minimal level. The graphics department has been heavily invested in.</p>
<p>The luxuries provided are meant to encourage productivity.</p>
<p>The company expects to grow this year, and for the next five years.</p>
<p>The company allows staff to work both in the office and remotely.</p>
<p>The company will be partnering with other small publishing businesses. Memorandums of Understanding have been signed regarding technical, memory and compute resources. Because of this, there is an expectation that the servers will be upgraded over the next five years.</p>
